## 🌟 Major Features

### Named NPCs (Oregon Trail Emotional Investment)
**File:** `hot-engine.js`
**Lines:** ~150

Your house is no longer abstract. You know the people who work for you:

- **Casso** (Senior Captain) - Records losses without comment
- **Pell** (Archivist) - Watches documents requiring second witness
- **Tucci** (Harbourmaster) - Remembers every unpaid gratuity

**Bond System:**
- 0-10 scale (visual: ■■■□□□□□□□)
- High bond (8+) unlocks special dialogue
- Strong allies reduce mortality risk (-2% per bond ≥8)
- NPCs can die — you'll feel it because you knew the name

**AI Integration:**
- Allies included in all AI prompts
- AI references allies by name in narratives
- "Casso's ship never arrived. You named him."

### Mortality Events (Oregon Trail Tension)
**File:** `hot-engine.js`
**Lines:** ~200

Death can happen anytime, not just at 65:

**Risk Calculation:**
- Base: 5% per turn
- +10% if reputation ≤ 2
- +5% if reputation ≤ 4
- +3% per hostile rival
- +5% if age ≥ 55, +10% if age ≥ 60
- -2% per strong ally (bond ≥ 8)

**AI-Generated Events:**
- Fever, assassination, shipwreck, poison, accident
- 3 choices per event with survival % (30%-95%)
- Costs marks, reputation, or both
- Death triggers generational handoff

**Example:**
> *"The physician arrives. The diagnosis is not optimistic."*
> - Pay for treatment (85% survival, -200 mk)
> - Rest and pray (55% survival, -1 rep)
> - Continue working (30% survival, +50 mk)

**File:** `hot-trading.js`
**Lines:** ~100

Trading is no longer shopping — it's strategy:

| Port | Saltfish | Wine | Alum | Tin | Risk |
|------|----------|------|------|-----|------|
| **Verantia** | 1.0x | 1.1x | 0.95x | 1.0x | Low |
| **Masso** | 1.2x | 0.8x | 1.1x | 0.9x | Medium |
| **Caldera** | 0.8x | 1.3x | 1.2x | 0.7x | Medium |

3. Profit: 0.8 mk per unit (vs 0-1 mk before)

**UI:**

- Active port highlighted

### Price System Overhauled
**File:** `hot-trading.js`

**Formula:**
```
Final Price = Base × Season × Port × Event × Variance
```

**Example Calculation:**
```
Saltfish in Northern Isles (Summer):
Base: 4 mk
Season: 0.7x (summer = cheap)
Port: 0.6x (Northern = saltfish capital)
Event: 1.0x (no event)
Variance: 0.95x (random ±15%)

Final: 4 × 0.7 × 0.6 × 1.0 × 0.95 = 1.6 mk
```

## 🎯 Design Philosophy

### Oregon Trail Spirit
> "Name → Love → Lose → Grieve"

**Implementation:**
- Named NPCs (Casso, Pell, Tucci)
- NPCs can die
- Player feels loss because they knew the name

### Paravia Spirit
> "I built something that outlasts me"

**Implementation:**
- Tangible buildings
- Persist across generations
- Ledger notes who built each

### Taipan! Spirit
> "Geographic arbitrage = profit"

**Implementation:**
- 4 ports with different prices
- Player chooses destination
- Strategic depth, not spreadsheet math

## 📝 Migration Notes

### Save File Compatibility
✅ **Backward Compatible**

Old saves work, but won't have:
- Named NPCs (generated on load)
- Buildings (can purchase going forward)
- Port history (defaults to Verantia)

### Browser Cache
**Hard refresh required** (Cmd+Shift+R)

Old cached files will break:
- Background won't change
- Ports won't appear
- Prices won't vary
